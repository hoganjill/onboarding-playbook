🚀 What to Expect on Day 1

Okta Activation

At approximately 8–9 AM local time, you'll receive your Okta activation email. Okta is Shopify's single sign-on and authentication tool. See the Day 1 setup instructions bookmarked in #help-it-onboarding.

Access Your Apps

Once Okta is set up, access Shopify's internal apps (Gmail, Slack, etc.) through the Okta dashboard.

Open Google Calendar

Your calendar is the source of truth for onboarding. On Day 1 you'll have a Start Here 🚀 invite with a Figjam link. The Figjam board opens the morning of your start date.

Follow the Figjam Board

The rest of your day is self-paced learning in the Startup Figjam board. You'll connect with your onboarding cohort and facilitator on Slack and attend any live sessions on your calendar.

🎓 Startup Onboarding

Startup Foundations (Remote)

Spans 2 days — ~24–30 hours of async modules plus 2 live debrief sessions. You'll be added to #startup-onboarding on Slack once your hardware is set up and Okta is verified.

📖 Startup Foundations on Vault

Startup Connections (In-Person)

An in-person onboarding experience — dive deeper into Foundations content, meet fellow new hires, build your Shopify community, and connect with leadership and local teams. Mandatory for all full-time permanent hires (except Merchant Support).

📖 Startup Connections on Vault · Travel & logistics

⚠️ Exceptions

If your first week overlaps with a Burst, event, or product review, you'll still have access to Startup Foundations and can revisit in Week 2. Reach out to your onboarding facilitator in #startup-onboarding to join the next cohort.
