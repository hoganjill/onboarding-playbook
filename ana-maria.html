Welcome, Ana-Maria

This playbook covers the most important information as you transition into your new role as Director, Corporate Data.

🚀 What to Expect on Day 1

Okta Activation

At approximately 8–9 AM local time, you'll receive your Okta activation email. Okta is Shopify's single sign-on and authentication tool. See the Day 1 setup instructions bookmarked in #help-it-onboarding.

Access Your Apps

Once Okta is set up, access Shopify's internal apps (Gmail, Slack, etc.) through the Okta dashboard.

Open Google Calendar

Your calendar is the source of truth for onboarding. On Day 1 you'll have a Start Here 🚀 invite with a Figjam link.

Follow the Startup Portal

The rest of your day is self-paced learning in the Startup portal. You'll connect with your onboarding cohort and facilitator on Slack.

🎓 Startup Onboarding

Startup Foundations (Remote)

Spans 2 days — ~24–30 hours of async modules plus 2 live debrief sessions. You'll be added to #startup-onboarding on Slack.

📖 Startup Foundations on Vault

Startup Connections (In-Person — Toronto)

A 2-day in-person onboarding experience. Meet fellow new hires, build your Shopify community, and connect with leadership. Includes fireside chats, team activities, pair programming, AI labs, and a pop-up market.

🗺️ Corporate Data

Your area spans 5 teams and approximately 34 people. The group's mission: "We're here to use data to maximize outcomes for Shopify."

📖 Nell's Big Rocks for 2026 · 📊 Townhall deck

Executive Insights 8

👤 Connor McCambridge · Manager
Builds and maintains data products for executive decision-making — CTO Dashboard, SLT reporting, weekly metric updates to C-suite + VPs, and forecasting models. Also drives the Data Foundation effort to close data gaps across the warehouse.
📋 Weekly update automation refactor (DSPy, evals)

Market Insights 7

👤 Anne Branchut · Manager
Focuses on external and third-party data — market dynamics, competitive landscape, merchant ecosystem. Runs surveys, tracks brand awareness, and produces research for brand, comms, and growth teams. Owns market research intake for the org.
📋 Merchant AI & Agentic Commerce research

Finance Data 8

👤 Andy Robinson · Manager
Builds data products for Shopify's finance org — revenue estimation, gross profit reporting, GMV forecasting, bookkeeping analytics, and lending data. Maintains critical financial models on the path for GMV calculation and month-end close.
📋 Local Currency GL Forecast · Payments Credit Risk Losses

Infrastructure Data 9

👤 Dan Cohen · Manager
Data science embedded in Product Eng — spanning platform infrastructure, ML/data platform, and developer productivity. Three strategic themes: Capacity Planning & Resiliency, Cost Efficiency (GCP + AI infra costs), and Developer Velocity. Leads BFCM data readiness.
📋 AI Cost Efficiency · Capacity engine for BFCM 2026 · Agentic GCP cost reporting

Corporate Data Engineering ~15

👤 Steven Wu · Manager
Data engineering embedded across all four Corporate Data sub-teams. Work organized around Executive Insights (CTO Dashboard, Semantic Layer), Finance (Rusbot for SOX compliance, revenue reporting), and Infrastructure (cost attribution, capacity planning). Formally reports into Analytics Engineering (Auggie Fisher) but embedded with Corporate Data day-to-day.
🚩 Note on team structure: Corporate Data Engineering formally sits within Analytics Engineering, but its Data Engineers are embedded across your sub-teams. Dan Cohen, Connor McCambridge, and Andy Robinson each have a cohort of DEs working alongside their PDS teams. In practice, Dan's Infrastructure Data team functions as ~16 people, even though only ~9 formally report to him. The benefit is flexibility — Steven can shift engineers where business need is highest.
